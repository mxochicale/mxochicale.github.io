Miguel P. Xochicale

I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Electronic, Electrical and Systems Engineering at University of Birmingham UK. I am interested in the field of Human-Robot Interaction and Human Activity Recognition. For my Ph.D. I am gaining deeper understanding of movement variability using Nonlinear Dynamics and Deep Learning Algorithms to create novel analysis and interpretation of time series. My background includes Electronics and Mechatronics as well as Digital Signal Processing. For more information check my updated CV or feel free to drop me an e-mail!

I do believe in Open Science and release pretty much all of my work in github.com/mxochicale. I tweet and retweet about Human-Robot Interaction, Nonlinear Dynamics and Deep Learning @_mxochicale
