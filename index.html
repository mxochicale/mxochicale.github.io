Miguel P. Xochicale

I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Electronic, Electrical and Systems Engineering at University of Birmingham UK. I am interested in the field of Human-Robot Interaction and Human Activity Recognition. For my Ph.D. I am specifically gaining deeper understanding of movement variability using Nonlinear Dynamics and Deep Learning Algorithms to create novel analysis and interpretation of signals collected through a network of wearable inertial measurement units.

Prior to pursuit my scientific endevours in my Ph.D., I developed a simple Dance Demo Interation with a service Robot at INAOE's robotics laboratory and worked in various Universities in Puebla, Mexico. My background includes Electronics and Mechatronics as well as Digital Signal Processing. For more information check my CV or feel free to drop me an e-mail!

I tweet and retweet about Human-Robot Interaciton, Deep Learning and Machine Rearning @_mxochicale and have a Google Scholar profile.
